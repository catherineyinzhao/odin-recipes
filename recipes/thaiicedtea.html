Thai Iced Tea

Rachel Cooks Thai, Thai Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup Thai tea mix
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup half and half cream
  • Cubed or crushed ice

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add the Thai tea mix, water, and sugar to a large pot. Heat until the sugar is completely dissolved, approximately 3-5 minutes. Turn off the heat and let the Thai tea steep for 30 minutes, until it's very dark reddish-orange in color.
  2. Use your Thai tea sock filter to strain the tea mix from the tea. Let the tea cool to room temperature and then chill in the fridge for several hours.
  3. Add ice to fill a tall glass. Pour approximately 1 cup of the chilled tea into the glass and then top with 3-4 tablespoons of half and half cream. Add a straw, stir, and enjoy!
